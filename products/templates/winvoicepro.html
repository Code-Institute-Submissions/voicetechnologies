WinVoicePro is Voice Technologies’ document workflow application

It has created 30 million documents and distributed 21 million electronically resulting in savings approaching £15m to our customers. Developed by Voice Technologies in collaboration with the NHS, WinVoicePro saves healthcare providers time and money. It is a complete end-to-end solution that allows users to create letters, reports and other documents and distribute them electronically or in print. It is highly adaptable, having been designed to work seamlessly alongside existing software to make the job of document creation easier and faster. It can be used on the move too with an optional mobile add-on that enables users to view, edit and authorise documents anywhere to avoid delays.

Key features:

  • Links with existing applications and databases to populate documents automatically
  • Turns digital dictations from WinScribe into documents for dispatch
  • Sends encrypted electronic documents for amendment, approval and dispatch
  • Omni-search facility makes it easy to search for and add recipients (v2.4)
  • Default recipient saves secretarial time by dispatching to the patient’s referring GP or defaulting to their registered GP (v2.4).

Key benefits:

  • Fills the costly void between digital dictation and distribution
  • Eliminates the time, cost and potential errors involved in re-keying data
  • Accelerates document turnaround times
  • Reduces resource, postage and printing costs
  • Mobile add-on saves time and makes use of existing hardware.
